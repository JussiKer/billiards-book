Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email and authentication session data.
  • Profile data: username and display name.
  • Gameplay data: matches, tournaments, scores, venues, comments, and event logs you create in the app.
  • Social data: friend requests, friendships, and tournament participation records.
  • Notification data: push token and platform (only after you grant notification permission).
  • Diagnostics: crash and error data when error reporting is enabled.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and keep accounts secure.
  • To provide match and tournament features, including sync across devices.
  • To show social features such as friends, comments, and live updates.
  • To send app notifications (for example tournament invites and comments).
  • To monitor reliability and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device and in our cloud backend (Supabase) to support account, sync, and social functionality. We use encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS) for data in transit.

4. Data Sharing and Visibility

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • In-app content is visible according to feature settings (for example friend visibility, tournament visibility, and participants).
  • We use service providers only to operate the app:
  • Supabase (authentication, database, realtime, edge functions).
  • Expo push service (push notification delivery).
  • Sentry (error and crash monitoring, if enabled).

5. Retention

We retain account and app data while your account is active, or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, associated account data is removed from our backend according to system constraints and legal obligations.

6. Your Choices and Controls

  • You can delete your account in the app (profile screen).
  • You can disable notifications at the device level.
  • You can remove local app data by uninstalling or clearing app storage.

7. Security Procedures

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect data, including authentication controls, row-level access rules in the backend, and restricted service-role usage for server-side operations. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to minimize risk.

8. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
