Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as email) for authentication.
  • Gameplay data (matches, tournaments, scores, player names, venues, event logs).
  • Friendship and social graph data inside the app.
  • Diagnostic data if crash/error reporting is enabled.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features and sync data across devices.
  • To support live tournament and match viewing.
  • To improve reliability, performance, and bug fixing.

3. Data Storage

Data may be stored locally on your device and in our cloud backend for synchronization and social features.

4. Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Public or friends-visible in-app content is shown according to app visibility settings.
  • Service providers are used only as needed to operate the app.

5. Retention

Data is retained while your account is active or as needed to provide the service, unless deletion is requested.

6. Your Choices

  • You can request deletion of your account and related data.
  • You can remove local app data by uninstalling or clearing app storage.

7. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.
